About us

Get to know us more

LYTECH Pharm is a leading monoclonal antibody production company founded in 2005.
Placing the patient and science at the heart of our development, we are always looking for innovative therapies to better care for patients and improve human health.
We offer more specific, innovative and personalized biomedicines with fewer side effects.

The origin
