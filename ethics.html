Responsabilities Ethics and transparency

For LYTECH Pharm, ethical and transparent behaviour is a daily commitment.

We are committed to respecting the principle of fair competition.
Anti-trust laws guarantee the free play of competition by prohibiting :

  • Any agreements with our competitors
  • Any agreements between suppliers and customers
  • Any abusive dominant position in the market

We are committed to the principle of integrity in our business relationships.

LYTECH Pharm does not tolerate any act of corruption.
Any attempt to illegally influence business partners is strictly prohibited.

We are committed to the principle of sustainability.

LYTECH Pharm is aware of its responsibility to protect the environment and the health and safety of all persons in contact with its products. The company develops and markets products and services that are beneficial to its users and their environment.

We are committed to fair and respectful working conditions.

No one within LYTECH Pharm shall be disadvantaged, favoured, harassed or ostracized because of race or origin, colour, nationality, religion, ideology, political or trade union membership, gender, age, appearance, sexual orientation or membership in protected groups in any country.

We undertake to cooperate with the authorities

Any information provided to the authorities must be provided in a complete, open, correct and comprehensible manner.
