Our goal

Actually, most monoclonal antibodies have been developed to fight cancers, immunological and inflammatory disorders.
However, with the emergence of antibiotic resistance, new viruses and the threat of bioterrorism, the development of anti-infective monoclonal antibodies is necessary.

Our goal is therefore to develop a new generation of therapeutic antibodies that target rare and serious diseases.
